Specialized K-4 Tutoring in Marin County

Providing individualized, multi-sensory tutoring for elementary students. Specializing in reading, writing, math, and learning strategies. Expert support for students with ADHD, Dyslexia and Learning Disabilities.

Personalized Instructional Intervention

ADHD Support

Specialized strategies and tools to help students with ADHD stay focused, organized, and engaged in their learning. Our structured approach builds executive functioning skills while maintaining enthusiasm for learning.

Dyslexia Intervention

Evidence-based, multi-sensory techniques specifically designed for students with dyslexia. We focus on building phonological awareness, decoding skills, and reading fluency through systematic instruction.

Learning Difficulties

Comprehensive support for various learning challenges, including processing speed, working memory, and comprehension difficulties. Individualized approaches that work with your child's unique learning style.

About Miss C
